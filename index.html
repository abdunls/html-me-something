East African Beauties!

Kigali, Gulu, & Malindi

Kigali, Rwanda

Kigali is the bustling capital city of Rwanda in eastern Africa. You can get a business up and running here in less than 24 hours! Here I learned about post-genocide restoration and peacebuilding.

Gulu, Uganda

Gulu is located in northern Uganda. Here I learned about post-conflict resolution. Remember Kony 2012? Well, that campaign was started in this area of the world.

Malindi, Kenya

Malindi, Kenya is located on the coast of Kenya on the Indian Ocean. My dad hails from here. Aren't their beaches beautiful?!
